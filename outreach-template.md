## Gmail Batch Rules

- Do not send 1,000 identical emails at once.
- Personalize the first line for each organization.
- Send in batches of 20-50 per day.
- Prefer program/contact addresses first, then named staff only when their email is publicly listed for partnerships/grants.
- Track every email sent in a CSV with date, organization, recipient, status, and follow-up date.
- Follow up once after 7-10 days, then stop unless they respond.
