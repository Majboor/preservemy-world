## Positioning Angles

### Emotional Public Movement

preservemy.world is a global memory preservation movement. It helps anyone turn footage of meaningful places into walkable 3D worlds, so culture, family memory, and everyday heritage can be experienced instead of forgotten.

### School and Student Ambassador Version

Students can help build the archive of the future. With only a phone video, schools and student ambassadors can preserve neighborhoods, cultural sites, campuses, family spaces, and local history as interactive 3D worlds.

### NGO and Museum Partnership Version

preservemy.world gives museums, NGOs, archives, and cultural organizations a simple Spatial AI pipeline for public heritage documentation. Partners can collect community footage, process it into explorable 3D worlds, and make vulnerable places easier to view, share, and teach.
