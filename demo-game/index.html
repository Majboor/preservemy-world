Recorded video to Spatial AI.

This world was captured from real footage, then converted into a walkable 3D scene.

Watch the route first.

The capture trajectory starts automatically so you can see the original recorded path.

Resolution is limited.

Compute and storage are limited in this demo, so some areas resolve better than others.

Fly it yourself.

Use the controls to leave the route and explore. If you spawn at an odd angle, hold Shift for boost and use Left/Right yaw to straighten your path.

ControlsDrag on the scene to look around. W/S or Fwd/Back move. A/D or Left/Right slide sideways. Arrow Left/Right or Yaw buttons rotate. Space/C or Up/Down change height. P or Home returns to spawn.
