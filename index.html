Why now

Record the world. Make it walkable.

The world is changing faster than we can document it. Photos and long videos matter, but future generations need a way to experience places, not just look at them.

Phones are everywhere, and people already carry footage of meaningful places.
3D reconstruction is finally becoming accessible to schools, communities, and families.
