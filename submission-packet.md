## Reusable Answers

### Synopsis, max 100 words

Culture should not disappear just because places do. This video presents preservemy.world, a youth-built prototype that turns simple phone footage into explorable 3D memories using AI spatial reconstruction and Gaussian Splats. From mosques, shrines and streets to homes, schools and travel memories, the project imagines a community-led archive where people can record a place, upload it, and preserve it as a world before it disappears.

### Inspiration, max 100 words

I am a 15-year-old student developer from Lahore and a traveller at heart. I love exploring places, but school, exams, family and startup work make travel difficult. The world is changing faster than we can document it, so I built this prototype to keep meaningful places close and help others preserve the spaces that shaped them.

### Bio, max 100 words

I am a 15-year-old student developer from Lahore and a traveller at heart. I build with AI, code and cloud tools to turn emotional ideas into working prototypes. This project grew from my love for culture, faith, travel and meaningful places, and from wanting future generations to experience memories instead of only watching videos.
